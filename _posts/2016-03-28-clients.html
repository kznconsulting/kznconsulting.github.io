We manage materials science websites as part of an effort to showcase materials science research funded by the National Science Foundation. With our dynamic design and original content, each of these websites serves as a platform to learn about the latest NSF-funded materials science research, news, and events, as well as connect researchers within the materials science community and beyond.

As the National Science Foundation continues to fund some of the most cutting-edge research in materials science, we highlight that effort on . We have built, designed, and manage this website—a product of NSF’s DMREF Program, short for Designing Materials to Revolutionize and Engineer Our Future.

Part of the National Science Foundation’s BIO-IOS Division, the NeuroNex program funds neuroscience research carried out by universities across the United States. We work with NSF as well as NeuroNex projects to disseminate that research by way of feature articles, news, and highlights and feature them on —a website built, designed, and maintained by KZN Consulting.
